Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 14, 2026

1. Information We Collect

Gold-Price.app is a free, informational website that displays live precious metal prices. We do not require account registration and do not collect personally identifiable information directly.

If you consent via our cookie banner, we use Google Analytics to collect anonymous usage data (pages visited, approximate location by country, device type, referring site) to help us understand how visitors use the site and improve it.

2. Cookies

We use cookies only after you give explicit consent. If you accept, Google Analytics sets cookies (_ga, _ga_*) to distinguish unique visitors. If you decline, no analytics cookies are set. You can clear your browser cookies at any time to revoke consent.

3. Third-Party Services

We use Google Analytics (Google LLC) for anonymous usage analytics and TradingView widgets to display price charts. These providers may set their own cookies subject to their privacy policies.

4. Data Retention

Google Analytics data is retained according to our Google Analytics settings. No personal data is stored on our own servers.

5. Your Rights (EU/UK/California)

Under GDPR, UK GDPR, and CCPA, you have the right to access, correct, or delete personal data we hold about you, and to withdraw consent at any time. Since we collect only anonymous analytics data, there is typically no personally identifiable information to retrieve. To request any action, contact us via the details on our site.

6. Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top will reflect any changes.
