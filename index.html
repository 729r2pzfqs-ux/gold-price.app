Frequently Asked Questions

What is the gold spot price?

The spot price is the current market price at which gold can be bought or sold for immediate delivery. It's determined by trading on commodity exchanges worldwide.

Why does gold price change?

Gold prices fluctuate based on supply and demand, inflation expectations, interest rates, currency movements (especially USD), and global economic/political uncertainty.

What is a troy ounce?

A troy ounce (31.1 grams) is the standard unit for measuring precious metals. It's slightly heavier than a regular ounce (28.35 grams).
